About Rahul PR

I'm Rahul PR — a writer, artist, designer, web developer, video editor, and content creator based in Kochi, Kerala, India. I wear many creative hats, but storytelling is at the heart of everything I do.

I write across multiple genres including children’s stories, romance, fantasy, horror, psychological tales, educational content, and science fiction. My diverse background in elementary education helps me craft stories that are both meaningful and memorable.

Born and raised in Kerala, I bring cultural depth and imagination into each project I take on—whether it’s a beautifully illustrated kids’ book, a compelling promotional video, or a fully functional website. Beyond writing, I design book covers, create social media content, build websites, and run storytelling workshops to spark creativity in young minds and aspiring creators.
